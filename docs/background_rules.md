# TradeLens Background Rules

- Use `background.md` for durable investor profile, base currency, market, broker, goals, strategy preferences, risk tolerance, exposure limits, forbidden instruments, mistakes, reminders, and analysis style.
- Ask the next missing durable background question when no update details are provided.
- Confirm extracted or inferred durable settings before updating `background.md`.
- Temporary trade context belongs in `trade.md` or an analysis record, not `background.md`.
- Preserve existing rules and keep change history where useful.
